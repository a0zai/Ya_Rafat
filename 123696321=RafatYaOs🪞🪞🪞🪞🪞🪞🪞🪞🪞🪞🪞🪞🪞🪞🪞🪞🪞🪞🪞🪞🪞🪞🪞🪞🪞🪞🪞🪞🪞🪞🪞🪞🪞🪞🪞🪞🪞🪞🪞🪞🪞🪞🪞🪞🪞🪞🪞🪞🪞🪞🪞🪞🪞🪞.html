Get Started

Getting Started

Learn how to quickly start using this framework. Include it via CDN or NPM, and begin building responsive sites in no time.

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/bootstrap@5.3.3/dist/css/bootstrap.min.css">

Customize

Easily customize colors, fonts, and components with our powerful variables and SASS support. Create your unique brand style effortlessly.

Layout

Leverage our flexible grid system and layout utilities to craft any design you imagine. Responsive, intuitive, and mobile-first.

Content

Style your typography, images, tables, and more with consistent, accessible content classes designed to look great on all devices.

Forms

Build accessible, attractive forms with our wide variety of form controls and layouts. Validation included out of the box.

Components

Choose from a vast library of pre-styled UI components: buttons, navbars, modals, carousels, alerts, and many more ready to drop in.

Helpers

Simplify your development with helper classes for positioning, spacing, text, colors, and more — all designed to be intuitive and flexible.

Utilities

Use our utility API to quickly tweak and adjust your designs without leaving your HTML. Control layout, spacing, colors, and much more.

Extend

Expand Bootstrap’s functionality with custom plugins, your own components, or third-party integrations. Fully open source and extensible.
